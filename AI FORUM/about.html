Head Of Department Message

It gives me immense pleasure to introduce AI PRABODHA, a dedicated forum that fosters learning, innovation, and collaboration in the field of Artificial Intelligence. As AI continues to reshape industries and revolutionize technology, it is crucial to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate this ever-evolving domain. AI PRABODHA serves as a platform for students to explore AI principles, engage in hands-on projects, and collaborate on groundbreaking ideas. Through workshops, hackathons, expert talks, and research initiatives, this forum aims to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical applications. I encourage all students to actively participate, learn, and contribute to the AI community. Let us work together to push the boundaries of AI and harness its potential for societal and technological advancement. Best wishes for an inspiring and innovative journey ahead!

Dr. Vikas G. Bhowate
Head, AI Department

    Information

    AI PRABODHA is a dynamic and innovative forum dedicated to fostering knowledge, research, and hands-on experience in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Established with the vision of empowering students and bridging the gap between theoretical AI concepts and real-world applications, the forum serves as a collaborative platform for students, researchers, and AI enthusiasts. Objectives of AI PRABODHA
  • Engage in practical AI projects, workshops, and coding sessions. Industry & Research Exposure
  • Collaborate with experts, researchers, and industry professionals. AI Innovations & Competitions
  • Participate in AI hackathons, challenges, and competitions. Skill Development
  • Learn AI tools, frameworks, and technologies through guided mentorship. Community Building
  • Connect with like-minded individuals to explore AI advancements.
